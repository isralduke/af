About Apex Focal

What is Experience Consulting?

Experience consulting removes the gap between your knowledge and your customers’ knowledge.

As a business owner you know every detail about whay you do. Your customers don’t know as much as you and they miss out. Apex Focal puts your knowledge into their experience by making it easy for them.

FAQ

Any Questions? Answered

Digital products come with a lot of questions. Let Apex Focal answer them for you.

Accessibility means that anyone can use your website with whatever device they need to use the web. For example, people with blindness use special software called a screen reader to help them use the web. Apex Focal can make sure that your vendor gave you a website that works with screen readers.
Business analysis is the homework which helps you to be sure that your website matches your company’s needs, workflow, and regulatory challenges. Apex Focal knows how to do this so that your website becomes a reliable tool.
User testing gets into viewpoint of your customers. They don’t know the ins and outs of your business, so they might become confused by why you need to do certain things the way you do. You’re close to the details of your business but your customers aren’t. Their confusion could cause problems. User testing removes their problems by using your website like they do.
Companies hire outside consultants all of the time to help them avoid blindspots. Apex Focal helps you avoid blindspots because we’re your third-party fact-checker.
